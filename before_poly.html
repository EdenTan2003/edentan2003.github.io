My Journey

Primary School

I graduated from West Grove Primary School in 2015 with a PSLE Score of 173. During this period, it was also my first time sitting for a major exam since this will tell me which stream I am going into. Unfortunately, my results didn't meet my expectations during the results release. But, I do know that the time spent in West Grove Primary School was a great one as I have met many classmates even though its been a few years after my graduation from West Grove Primary School.

Secondary School

During this period, I was enrolled into Westwood Secondary School in the Normal-Academic Stream through the Direct-Admission Exercise. My time in Westwood Secondary was when I started to excell in my studies. On the other hand, I can't be more thankful to the people that I have met especially my school teachers as they have helped me not only in my academics but also in my well-being. I have also joined Bowling as my CCA, partly the reason why I DSA into this school. Being appointed Vice-Captain during my 3rd Year in Bowling was the best thing that happened to me, as it allows me to learn leadership and communication skills.

ITE College West

After graduating from Westwood Secondary School with a score of 8 (With CCA Points) in my Normal-Academic Studies, I chose to pursue IT Applications Development in ITE College West. During my 2 years in this course, I have learnt many useful skills to create Web and Mobile Applications using Javascript, PHP, C#, Swift and many more. Also, I am able to apply skills that I have learnt in my ITE studies into SP DIT. I graduated from my course with a GPA of 4 and I have been given many opportunities to try out new things. At the end of the day, I have graduated with a Certificate Of Merit, Adobe Book Award, and Top Graduate Award.
