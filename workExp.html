We are passionate about the work we do and the wonderful communities where we work. My role in the company

I was given the opportunity to join the business side during my first few weeks at Octopus8 and coming from a school that does Information Technology I can say that I actually enjoy being able to experience tasks that I am new to. This is so that my learning experience here at Octopus8 is not only limited to learning technical stuff but both sides. During my second half of my internship, I also got the opportunity to join the technical side, I was tasked to do things that I have learnt in school and I am able to apply it.

What I have learnt

Through my internship experience at Octopus8, I have learnt many things that I would not have learnt in school. I have learnt the different solutions that Octopus8 offers to their clients and how we can configure and meet the clients needs using open source and integrated solutions. My experience at Octopus8 was a fruitful one, such that I have offered to come back to work for the company as the company is expanding and I would like to be part of the expansion where I was able to learn more things.
