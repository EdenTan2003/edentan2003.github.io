More About Me

My Values

Some of my values include Kindness, Honesty and last but not least Compassion. As the value Compassion suggest, I have always had a strong concern for the people around me. Be it whether they are struggling with something in their lives or having issues with their work, I would always try my best to help them with the best of my ability as I believe what goes around comes around.

My interest include Badminton and Bowling. Bowling has always been a interest for me as I have always wanted to become as good as the professional bowlers that I know of. My Personality

After doing my R.I.A.S.A.C during my ECG lesson in DIT, I have gotten C - Conventional, I - Investigative and S - Social. This shows that I am someone who likes to follow a routine and being organized, as I dislike things that are being disorganized. Furthermore, being a conventional person, I like to work with data and numbers which explains why I have chosen DIT as my course in Singapore Polytechnic.
