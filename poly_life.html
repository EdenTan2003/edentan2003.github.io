MY LIFE IN SINGAPORE POLYTECHNIC...

My Current Experience in

Singapore Polytechnic

My time in Singapore Polytechnic has been a fruitful one, as I am able to meet many new people that I have never met before. On the other hand, being able to meet my classmates during the Freshman Orientation Program was one of the exciting things that happened so far as I get to meet people from different backgrounds. My time in SP is currently going well as I manage to know all of my classmates well during the first few weeks of school.

School of Computing

Being in School of Computing allows me to meet people who are strong in specific fields, and being able to know more about their fields allows me to broaden my knowledge in fields that I am unfamiliar with. Furthermore, being in SOC can help me to learn more skills in creating Websites, Apps, including Hardware/Software components which is something I am new to.

Find Out More

SP Bowling

I have decided to join SP Bowling as my CCA knowing that I have a strong interest in Bowling. Furthermore, being in SP Bowling would allow me to improve my skills, including the chance to represent SP in the POLITE Bowling Tournament which consist of all the 6 Polytechnic's and ITE. I was able to meet the captains, vice-captains, and coaches during my trials which allowed me to know more about the people who are in charge of this CCA.

My TimeTable

This is my current Year 1, Semester 1 Timetable. For my class timetable, we do not have any free days compared to other classes which can be quite tiring. However, we are given longer lunch breaks which allows us to work on our assignments after we are done with lunch. We can also spent the time in places like Hilltop Haven or even the SP Library when we have longer lunch breaks.

In my diploma, I am currently learning Web-Development Skills, Basic Javascript, Computer Fundamentals and also skills like Presentation and Researching.
